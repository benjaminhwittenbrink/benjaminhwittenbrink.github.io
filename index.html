Welcome!

I am currently studying Economics and Mathematics at Stanford (Class of '22). I am interested in applying a quantitative, mathematical, and data-driven lens to better understand issues of economic, political, and social importance.

For the past year, I was on a leave of absence, interning at QuantCo, a data science startup working at the intersection of econometrics and machine learning. During the summer of 2020, I worked as a summer analyst on the New York Federal Reserve's Financial Intermediation Research team and as a research assistant for Mark Duggan and Gopi Goda at the National Bureau of Economic Research (and previously at the Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research). In 2019, I interned with QuantCo's Zurich team. Before that, I worked on a neuroeconomics project with the Apex Lab and as a research assistant within John List's lab, both at the University of Chicago.
